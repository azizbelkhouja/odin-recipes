Real Hummus

Ingredients:

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can garbanzo beans
  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • black pepper to taste

Step By Step:

  1. Chop garlic in a blender.
  2. Pour garbanzo beans into the blender, reserving about 1 tablespoon for garnish.
    Add reserved liquid, lemon juice, tahini, and salt to the blender. Blend until creamy and well mixed.
  3. Transfer mixture to a medium serving bowl. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle pepper
    over the top. Garnish with reserved garbanzo beans.
